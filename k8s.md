### k8s

Something may eventually replace Kubernetes as an implementation detail, but the pattern of `declarative workloads` plus `reconciliation loops` is expected to persist.

Extensibility via `CRDs` and `controllers` enabled diverse developer-experience layers (GitOps, operators, PaaS platforms) to emerge on top of a stable core.

### ai

AI is better as a `front-end` for `intent-based APIs`: users express goals (“deploy this app safely”), and `deterministic` systems (Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD) implement them.​
